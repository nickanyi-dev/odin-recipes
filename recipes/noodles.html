Easy Egg Noodles

Easy Peasy Noodles

This egg noodle recipe is basic and old-fashioned, just like grammy used to make! Try them next time you prepare your favorite soup or stew. Don't forget that fresh pasta cooks much quicker than dried! You may use a dough hook attachment on your electric mixer or your hands to knead the pasta dough.

Ingredients

  • 2 ½ cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 pinch salt
  • 2 eggs, beaten
  • ½ cup milk
  • 1 tablespoon butter
